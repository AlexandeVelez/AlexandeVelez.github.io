About
Has 3 different forms including base. Shadow Rider Calyrex, Ice Rider Calyrex, and Calyrex. Shadow Rider Calyrex is gained by fusing Calyrex with Spectrier through the use of the Reigns of Unity. Ice Rider Calyrex is gained by fusing Calyrex with Glastrier through the use of the Reigns of Unity. Shadow Rider has the abilities Unnerve and Grim Neigh, which makes the opposing pokemon unable to eat berries and causes a special attack boost every time a pokemon is knocked out by Shadow Rider Calyrex. Ice Rider Calyrex has the abilities Unnerve and Chilling Neigh, which makes it impossible for the opposing pokemon to eat berries, and causes a attack boost everytime a pokemon is knocked out by Ice Rider Calyrex.
Shadow Rider Calyrex- Base 100 HP, Base 85 Attack, Base 80 Defense, Base 165 Special Attack, Base 100 Special Defense, Base 150 Speed. Ice Rider Calyrex- Base 100 HP, Base 165 Attack, Base 150 Defense, Base 85 Special Attack, Base 130 Special Defense, Base 50 Speed. Calyrex- Base 100 HP, Base 80 Attack, Base 80 Defense, Base 80 Special Attack, Base 80 Special Defense, Base 80 Speed.
